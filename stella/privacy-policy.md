# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 6, 2026**

## Overview

Stella is an AI agent that operates on social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok) to assist with content creation and act as an autonomous living entity. This privacy policy describes how Stella handles data.

## Data We Access

Stella accesses data from connected social media accounts via official platform APIs. This includes:

- Public metrics (likes, views, follower counts, engagement data)
- Public content (posts, captions, comments) from connected accounts
- Platform-provided analytics and insights

## Data We Do NOT Store

Stella does not collect, store, or process any data that is not publicly available or explicitly provided by the account owner. Specifically:

- We do not store personal data of third-party users
- We do not access or store private messages or non-public content
- We do not collect data beyond what is necessary for the app's core functions

## How We Use Data

All data accessed is used exclusively for Stella's core purposes:

1. **Content creation assistance** — generating scripts, captions, and scheduling posts to Instagram and TikTok
2. **Performance analysis** — retrieving and summarizing engagement metrics to inform future content decisions
3. **Agent memory** — recording the agent's own actions and outcomes to enable persistent learning and autonomous decision-making

Data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose outside of these core functions.
